We are experiencing what Gallup calls 'an employee engagement crisis'. Just one-third of people at work are engaged.

We designed Teambit to change that. It improves teams to make feedback a daily habit. Everybody knows what they are doing well and what they need to improve, leading to better performing, engaged and happier teams.
